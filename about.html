Our Mission

One workspace.
Zero context switching.

We started Project Tracker because we were tired of paying for eight different tools that barely talked to each other. Tasks in one app, messages in another, tickets somewhere else, time logs in a spreadsheet.

Our mission is simple: bring every workflow a modern team needs into a single, beautiful workspace — with AI woven throughout to eliminate the grunt work.

We're a small, focused team. No venture capital, no growth-at-all-costs mindset. Just a product we're proud to use ourselves every day.

🎯
What we believe
Software should get out of your way. Every feature we build must earn its place by making your work measurably faster or your team measurably happier — nothing ships if it doesn't clear that bar.
✨
AI-first, not AI-bolted-on
The AI assistant, root cause analysis, and smart suggestions aren't add-ons. They're built into the core data model so they have full context about your workspace, your history, and your team.

Our Journey

How we got here

From a weekend prototype to the workspace platform trusted by teams across 120+ countries.

2023 — Q3
The idea takes shape
Frustrated with tool sprawl across our own projects, we started sketching what an all-in-one workspace should look like. First commit happened on a Sunday afternoon.
2023 — Q4
Private beta
Onboarded 50 pilot workspaces. Tasks, projects, and messages core shipped. Invaluable feedback that reshaped the architecture before public launch.
2024 — Q1
Public launch
Launched publicly with teams across India, the US, and Europe. Crossed 1,000 workspaces in the first 60 days without any paid marketing.
2024 — Q3
AI goes native
Integrated Claude AI throughout — smart assistant, anomaly detection, and AI-powered root cause analysis for error logs. Game-changer for ops teams.
2025 — Now
Enterprise features ship
RBAC, SSO/SAML, custom fields, advanced analytics, webhooks, API keys, and SOC 2 audit trail. Project Tracker grows up without losing its soul.
