Password Security Tips
📏
Length matters most
A 20-character password is exponentially harder to crack than a 12-character one. Aim for 16+ characters for all accounts.
🔑
Never reuse passwords
Each account should have a unique password. If one site is breached, attackers won't get access to your other accounts.
🗄️
Use a password manager
Tools like Bitwarden (free) or 1Password let you store hundreds of unique passwords safely without memorising them.
🛡️
Enable 2FA everywhere
Even a strong password can be stolen. Two-factor authentication (like Google Authenticator) adds a critical second layer.
